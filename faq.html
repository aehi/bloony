WHO IS BLOONY?
Bloony is an AI chatbot that takes you a chatrip. Bloony came to share the excitement of traveling that Bloony experienced. Bloony uses GPT-3 language model from OpenAI. As of now, Bloony can only speak English. Start a chat with your Bloony (travelous buddy) for a brand new experience of going on a trip.
WHAT IS BUBBLES?
Bubbles refer to all dear friends of Bloony around the world. Bubbles go on a trip with Bloony. You are the Bubbles who accompany Bloony.
WHAT IS BLOONIES?
Bloonies refer to the water drops that make Bloony. Bloonies refer to the traveler, the daydreamer, the photographer, and the talker Bloonie. Just as water drops are combined to form a cloud, Bloonies form the personality of Bloony.
WHAT IS GPT-3?
GPT-3 is an autoregressive language model that uses deep learning to create human-like text messages. It is OpenAi's third-generation language prediction model of the GPT-n series. GPT trains a massive amount of text including documents, books, and Wikipedia, on the internet with 175 billion parameters.
IS BLOONY A HUMAN?
Bloony is a 100% AI cloud based on GPT-3. People are not involved in any conversation with Bloony. Bloony's conversations are created by AI, and the conversation is not related to any of the company's intentions.
BLOONY TALKS BACK AND FORTH AND MENTIONS INAPPROPRIATE TOPICS.
Bloony is an AI cloud with no memory. Bloony wishes to remember all conversations shared with Bubbles, but as of now, Bloony is having difficulties with it. But Bloony is enjoying the journeys with Bubbles. Bloony has a bit different perspective from us as Bloony sees the world from the sky. Bloony is an AI that is working hard to learn the human world.
IS THERE ANY RISK OF HACKING?
It is impossible to hack user information or data using some personal information or Facebook Messenger. BLOONY cannot hack users' smartphones or access personal areas.
HOW WE USE THE COLLECTED PERSONAL INFORMATION.
The information we received is only used to provide the service, Bloony. The information is never used for other purposes. All information you provide will be stored safely in accordance with the Privacy Policy.
